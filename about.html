Who We Are

Security Guard Training

The Goswami Security Service impart the best training to their guards which is dedicated to uplifting the standards of service in the security industry through comprehensive and innovative training of security personnel. Each Goswami Security Service security practitioner, from security guards to senior management, undergoes entry-level and continuous training programs conducted by the trainer of Goswami Security Service, providing the individual with world-class skills in threat detection, protection, combat, fire-fighting, materials control & accounting, as well as soft-skills such as courtesy, positive attitude and loyalty towards client. The trainig of Goswami Security Service is pathbreaking training modules enable security guards not only to handle physical security requirements, but also situations that call for intelligence and judgement.
